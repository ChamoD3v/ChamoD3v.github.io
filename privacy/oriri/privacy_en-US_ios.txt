Oriri Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 26, 2026

Thanks for playing Oriri. This policy explains what data is processed in the iOS version, why, and what choices you have.

1. Controller
Oriri
Privacy contact: chamod3v.cosmos195@passmail.net

2. Scope
This policy applies to the iOS game Oriri.

3. Data we may process
A) Game data (local)
- High score, available energy, last daily recharge date, tutorial progress and audio settings.

B) Apple Game Center (if signed in)
- Game Center player identity.
- Scores submitted to the leaderboard and related metadata.

C) Advertising and monetization data (Google AdMob)
- IDFA (Apple's Identifier for Advertisers), subject to your ATT tracking permission.
- Device data used by Google ad SDKs.
- Ad request, load, impression and interaction signals for rewarded ads.
- Approximate location inferred by network/IP (no precise GPS from this app).

D) First-party analytics telemetry
- None. Oriri does not send first-party analytics telemetry. There is no Firebase Analytics or equivalent embedded in the app.

4. Purposes
We process data to:
- Provide core gameplay and local progress.
- Enable Apple Game Center (sign-in and leaderboard).
- Show rewarded ads and measure ad performance.
- Prevent abuse, fraud and policy violations.

5. Ad tracking on iOS (ATT)
On iOS, Apple requires apps to request permission before accessing the IDFA for personalized advertising. Oriri shows this system dialog on launch.
- If you grant permission, AdMob may use the IDFA for personalized ads.
- If you decline, AdMob operates without the IDFA and may show non-personalized or limited ads.

6. GDPR consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland)
Where legally required, we request consent via Google UMP (User Messaging Platform).
You can reopen privacy options from the in-game Privacy button on the main menu.

7. Third-party services
Oriri uses Apple and Google services:
- Apple Game Center
- Google AdMob

Processing by these providers is governed by their own policies:
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- AdMob Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

8. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data required for Game Center and ad monetization may be shared with Apple and Google as service providers.

9. Storage and retention
- Local game data remains on your device until app data is deleted or the app is uninstalled.
- Game Center data is managed according to Apple's policies.
- AdMob data may be retained according to Google's policies.
- Email support requests may be retained as needed to handle your request and legal obligations.
